About Hydrowate

For years we've been revolutionizing wastewater management with innovative solutions that are smarter, easier, and sustainable.

Reliable STP Solutions That Protect Projects — And Your Reputation


Hydrowate Projects Pvt. Ltd. is a Bengaluru-based company specializing in wastewater management and waste-to-energy solutions. We design, build, and manage systems that transform waste into reusable resources — reducing costs, ensuring compliance, and protecting the environment.

Our innovative approach eliminates conventional blowers and diffusers, delivering superior results with unmatched efficiency. We serve residential complexes, educational institutions, commercial spaces, and industrial facilities across India.

Our Services

Revolutionary hybrid technology for efficient sewage treatment

Bio-Jet (Advanced SBR)

Advanced Jet Aeration technology — injects pressurized air and recirculated wastewater through jet nozzles, creating fine bubbles for maximum oxygen transfer.

  • High oxygen transfer
  • No blowers or diffusers
  • No moving parts inside tank
